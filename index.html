During college period, I always think that I am so lucky to get the chance to learn what I'm interested in: computer and language. I have Bachelor's Degree in English, and I have English TEM-8 certificate. English can be used as working language. I have ability to handle human resource affairs for foreign employees. I am a detail-oriented HR Specialist in an offshore engineering enterprise. I can deal with various computer/smartphone problems in Windows, macOS, iOS and Android.

I am a geek curious about any new things: currently I'm studying Natural Language Processing (NLP), Python programming and other languages like Russian, French, Koean, etc.
